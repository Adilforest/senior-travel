Explore travel's softer side

Our Comfort trips are all about adventure, with a higher level of comfort than our Original and Basix trips. These slower-paced trips are built to smooth some of travels rougher edges, meaning you’ll be on private transport more often, the pace will be more relaxed and your accommodation will be a touch above the essentials. You’ll also be treated to more included meals and leader-led activities, with a focus on embracing all things local.

What to Expect

Local Leader

The passion of our local leaders is what makes our trips so special. You’ll have a highly experienced leader with invaluable local knowledge who’ll be there for you every step of the way.

Group Activities

Our Comfort-style trips follow a more structured itinerary and feature more included activities. This leaves you to reap the benefits of more time in the company of a local leader – and their local knowledge.

Some Meals

Several meals are included – all of your breakfasts along the way and then perhaps visits to some of our favourite restaurants with your group to get a taste of the region.
